# Privacy Policy – Don’t Touch Red

Don’t Touch Red does not collect, store, or share personal information.

The app does not require user accounts and does not collect names, email addresses, or other identifying information.

The app uses third-party advertising services (Google AdMob). AdMob may collect limited information such as device identifiers and ad interaction data in order to provide and improve advertisements. This data is collected and processed in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

**Google Privacy Policy:**  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

The developer has no access to, and does not control, the data collected by third-party advertising services.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer.
